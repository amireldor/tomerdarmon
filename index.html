In the beginning...

I was born to an artistic family where my father is an international bronze sculptor and my mum is a photographer, which deeply influenced my creative journey. Growing up surrounded by art, I developed a passion for expressing myself through sound, making my music a natural continuation of the creativity and artistic inspiration that filled my life. I didn't listen to rock music until I reached the age of 12 years old when I found the band Queen which gave me a whole new perspective on music, especially when it comes to compositions, melodies and production. That moment changed my future later on when I was traveling to find the best new place to settle in order to make music.

My band - LOUD n' CLEAR

5 guys that want to play Rock n' Roll - this is how I would describe the band LOUD n' CLEAR. A hard rock band influenced by the 80's glam metal but most of all, it's the heart and soul that is being put into another dimension. With luck, faith and a lot of hard work that each one of us invested, we managed to play in several big metal/hard rock festivals, won 1st place in a bands contest, opening for Iron Maiden's singer Paul Dianno and Ron Bumblefoot, who played guitar with Guns n' Roses at that time.

"Playing With Thunder" - The Album

The band decided to go on separate ways in 2005, but after demands by loyal fans that wanted to see the band alive as in the old days, the band came back together but with a new line-up and recorded an album in 2012 called "Playing with Thunder". Immediately after releasing the album, the band went on a tour to promote the album with the new line-up, which brought freshness with modern sound aside to the bands old spirit. The album was sold out in Germany but gained more success in Sweden on radio stations like Bandit Rock and was played in hard rock clubs.
